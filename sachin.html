Sachin Tendulkar

🏏Introduction🏏

He is widely regarded as one of the greatest cricketers of all time, and is the holder
of several world records, including being the all-time highest run-scorer in both ODI and Test cricket,
receiving the most player of the match awards in international cricket, and being the only batsman to 
score 100 international centuries.
